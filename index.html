How to play?

Power play!

In this mythical twist on rock-paper-scissors, you'll choose between three powerful creatures: the mighty Dragon(Fire), the multi-headed Hydra(Water), and the majestic Griffin!(Air)


Each creature has a unique hand gesture as well as power

  • Dragon: Fist (Strength)
  • Hydra: Open hand (Five finger represents multiple heads)
  • Griffin: Peace sign (Two finger repesents combined power of a lion and bird)

To win, choose the creature that overpowers your opponent's based on the following:

  • defeats :Dragon fiery breath could pose a significant harm to Griffin aerial abilities.
  • defeats :Hydra mulitiple venomous head allow it to continue the fight and hydra venomous bite weaken the dragon over time.
  • defeats : Griffin agility and aerial prowess allow it to attack from different angles and potentially avoid hydra bites.
